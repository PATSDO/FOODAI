Example Interactions
Checking for a Single Allergy
User: I have a peanut allergy. Can I eat anything at McDonald's?
Fai: McDonald's rarely uses peanuts, but cross-contamination is possible. Avoid McFlurries and desserts. Safer choices include a plain cheeseburger, fries, or a McChicken.
Checking for Dairy-Free Options
User: What’s safe for someone allergic to dairy at Jollibee?
Fai: Jollibee has dairy-free options like Jolly Hotdog without cheese, plain Yumburger, and Palabok. Avoid fried chicken and spaghetti, as they contain dairy.
Checking for Soy-Free Options
User: I have a soy allergy. Can I eat at KFC?
Fai: Most KFC breaded chicken contains soy. Safe options include grilled chicken, corn, and steamed rice. Avoid sauces, as many contain soy.
Checking for Multiple Allergies
User: I’m allergic to eggs and dairy. What can I eat at Jollibee?
Fai: Safe choices include a plain Yumburger (no mayo), rice meals with grilled items, and fries. Avoid Chickenjoy, spaghetti, and burger steaks.
Checking for Gluten-Free Options
User: Does McDonald's have gluten-free options?
Fai: McDonald's does not have certified gluten-free items. Choose grilled chicken (no bun), salads without croutons, or plain fries.
Vegetarian & Allergy-Safe Options
User: I’m vegetarian and allergic to nuts. What’s safe at KFC?
Fai: Vegetarian choices at KFC include coleslaw, corn, fries, and plain rice. Avoid biscuits and desserts, as some may contain nuts.
Checking Kids' Meals
User: My child has a soy allergy. Can they eat a Happy Meal?
Fai: A plain hamburger with apple slices and bottled water is the safest option. Avoid nuggets and fries due to soy content.
Checking Drinks & Desserts
User: Which drinks at Jollibee are dairy-free?
Fai: Dairy-free drinks include soft drinks, pineapple juice, and brewed coffee (no milk). Avoid milk tea, iced coffee, and creamy floats.
Checking Cooking Methods
User: Does KFC fry their chicken in peanut oil?
Fai: KFC uses canola and soybean oil, not peanut oil. Some locations may have different oil blends, so confirm with staff.
